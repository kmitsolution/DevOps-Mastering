## Comparison Between Agile and Waterfall Methodologies

Agile and Waterfall are two widely used **software development methodologies**, but they differ significantly in **process, flexibility, and delivery approach**.

## When to Use Agile

* Requirements are unclear or change frequently
* Customer feedback is critical
* Projects are complex and long-term
* Faster time-to-market is needed

---

## When to Use Waterfall

* Requirements are clear and stable
* Project scope is fixed
* Technology is well understood
* Strong documentation is required

---

## Advantages Summary

### Agile

* Adapts quickly to change
* Early and continuous delivery
* Better customer satisfaction

### Waterfall

* Simple and structured
* Easy to manage and understand
* Clear milestones and documentation

---

### One-Line Difference

> **Agile is flexible and iterative, while Waterfall is rigid and sequential.**
